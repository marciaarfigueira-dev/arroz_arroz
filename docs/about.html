This study applies the Environmental Footprint (EF) 3.0 Method (adapted), v1.03, using the EF 3.0 characterisation, normalisation, and weighting sets. Developed by the European Commission, the EF method provides a harmonised framework for assessing environmental performance across supply chains and is used in Product Environmental Footprint (PEF) and Organisation Environmental Footprint (OEF) studies.

At the characterisation stage, inventory flows (e.g., kg CO₂, kg NOx, m³ water, MJ fossil energy) are converted into midpoint impact indicators using EF 3.0 characterisation factors.

After characterisation, EF 3.0 applies normalisation and weighting factors to express all impact categories on a common scale. This produces the single-score results in micro Points (µPt), allowing comparison and aggregation across impact categories.

Methane correction

IPCC Tier 1 (rice, flooded)

Methane emissions for flooded rice were calculated with IPCC Tier 1 (Equation 5.2): EF_daily = EFc × SFw × SFp × SFo; CH₄ (kg/ha) = EF_daily × t_days; CO₂e (kg/ha) = CH₄ (kg/ha) × 28; CH₄ SS (nPt/ha) = CO₂e (kg/ha) × 0.78 (EF3 single score factor).

Assumptions: EFc = 1.56; SFw = 1; SFp = 1.22; SFo = 2.539 (from straw + CFOA); t_days = 130.

Nitrous oxide correction

IPCC equations (direct + indirect N₂O)

1. Activity data and system boundaries
Annual, per farm/DMU. Includes synthetic N (FSN), organic N (FON), crop residues (FCR, mainly rice straw). No grazing deposits (FPRP = 0) and no land-use change mineralisation (FSOM = 0). Inputs aggregated per year and DMU.

2. Direct N₂O emissions (Eq. 11.2)
N₂Odirect-N = Σ (FSNᵢ + FONᵢ) × EF₁ᵢ + FCR × EF₁
Rice-specific EF₁: EF1FR = 0.003 (continuous flooding) or 0.005 (mid-season drainage/AWD). Residue EF₁ = 0.01 kg N₂O–N/kg N. Straw N ≈ 0.5% of dry matter; straw yield ≈ 1.5 × grain yield.
Convert to N₂O: N₂Odirect = N₂Odirect-N × 44/28.

3. Indirect N₂O (volatilisation & leaching)
Volatilisation (Eq. 11.9): N₂Ovolatil-N = [Σ FSNᵢ × FracGASFᵢ + FON × FracGASM] × EF₄; with FracGASF: urea 0.20–0.25, NPK 0.07, AN/CAN 0.03; FracGASM = 0.20; EF₄ = 0.01. Convert to N₂O with 44/28.
Leaching (Eq. 11.10): N₂Oleach-N = (FSN + FON + FCR) × Frac_LEACH-(H) × EF₅; Frac_LEACH-(H) = 0.30; EF₅ = 0.0075 (or 0.01 if using 2019 refinement). Convert to N₂O with 44/28.

4. CO₂ from urea hydrolysis (Eq. 11.13)
CO₂ = (Murea × EFCO₂-C) × 44/12, with EFCO₂-C = 0.20 kg C/kg urea.

5. Conversion to EF 3.0 climate
Characterisation: N₂O → 273 kg CO₂eq/kg N₂O; CO₂ → 1 kg CO₂eq/kg CO₂. Climate Change impact = N₂Odirect + N₂Ovolatil + N₂Oleach (all ×273) + CO₂ from urea. Reported in kg CO₂eq and, where relevant, converted to EF single-score (µPt) via EF 3.0 factors.
