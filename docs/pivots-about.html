About the Pivot Tables

12 farmers across 3 seasons. Pivot views to explore inputs and operations before LCA.

Context

Scope

Farmers & seasons

Data covers 12 farmers over 3 production seasons (2022–2024). Each row in the pivots represents an operation or input event for a farmer-season.

Farmer C7 and NT1 did not produce rice in the year 2022.

Inputs & operations

What’s included

  • Crop protection: herbicide, fungicide, insecticide doses; enemies; kg/ha and kg/t.
  • Sowing: seed rates (kg/ha, kg/t), operation type (row/broadcast), equipment, varieties.
  • Fertilisation: N, P, K, SO₄ rates (kg/ha, kg/t) by operation (basal/topdressing), equipment, product.
  • Machines: equipment used, area worked (repetitions normalized), area per tonne.
  • Water: m³/ha and m³/t water use per farmer-season.

Data sources

Mastersheets

All pivots are built from the operations mastersheets in the pivot_tables folder (e.g., crop protection, sowing, fertilisation, machines, water). Numbers are already normalized (kg/ha, kg/t, m³/ha, m³/t) where applicable.

The data was exclusively scrapped from the farmers field notebooks, and normalsied.

Navigation

How to use

  • Open a pivot table from the home page.
  • Use filters (season, farmer, operation, equipment, product) to narrow scope.
  • Pivot tables show aggregated stats plus detailed rows; exports feed the LCA calculators.
